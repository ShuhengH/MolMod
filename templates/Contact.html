Associate Professor Huang Shuheng

Computer-Aided Drug Design Expert

Her main research areas include computer-aided drug design, computational chemistry, and bioinformatics. She focuses on the application of artificial intelligence and theoretical simulation in new drug screening and molecular recognition mechanisms. She has published more than 30 SCI papers, presided over multiple national and provincial-level projects, and serves as a reviewer for multiple journals and as a young editorial board member of the Journal of Hainan University. She has extensive research experience and profound academic achievements in drug molecular design and computational chemistry.
